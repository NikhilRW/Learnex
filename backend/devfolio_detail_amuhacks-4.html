AMUHACKS 4.0 – Code, Innovate, and Solve Real-World Problems!

🚀 Fully Online | Open to All | Prize Pool: ₹15,000 + Paid Internship Opportunities + E-Certificates

Welcome to AMUHACKS 4.0, the fourth edition of our highly anticipated hackathon! This time, we are going fully online, making it more accessible, resource-efficient, and time-saving for participants across the globe. No travel hassles, no logistical barriers—just pure innovation from the comfort of your home. Collaborate with like-minded tech enthusiasts, build game-changing solutions, and compete for exciting prizes!

Why Join AMUHACKS 4.0?

✅ Fully Online – Participate from anywhere, saving time and resources
✅ Diverse Tracks – Work on real-world challenges in impactful domains
✅ Attractive Prizes – Win from a ₹15,000 prize pool
✅ Paid Internship - Internship Opportunity from Our Gold Sponsor
✅ E-Certificates for All – Recognition for every participant
✅ Network & Learn – Connect with experts and fellow innovators

Tracks:

🔹 Education – Create tech-driven solutions to enhance learning experiences
🔹 Community Empowerment – Build tools that uplift communities and drive social impact
🔹 Cyber Crime Prevention – Strengthen cybersecurity and digital safety measures
🔹 Smart City Development – Innovate for sustainable and tech-enabled urban living
🔹 Healthcare Innovation – Revolutionize healthcare with technology
🔹 Finance – Develop smart financial solutions for better accessibility and security

Prizes:

🏆 Winner – ₹7,000 + Internship Opportunity
🥈 Runner-Up – ₹5,000
🥉 Second Runner-Up – ₹3,000
🎖️ E-Certificates – Awarded to all participants

AMUHACKS 4.0 is more than just a competition—it’s a platform to showcase your skills, tackle pressing challenges, and bring your ideas to life. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced coder, this hackathon is your chance to innovate and make an impact!

🚀 Are you ready to hack your way to glory? Stay tuned for registration details!

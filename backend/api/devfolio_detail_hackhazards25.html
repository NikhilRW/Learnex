Build in India, for the world.!

One of India's largest and premier community-run hackathons, makes a comeback, this time with much more surprises in store.

HACKHAZARDS '25 celebrates India and her developers in its latest edition. Join us as we embark on this journey of bringing together the best of the country and not only from top cities/colleges but also from each and every corner of our country.

WHY JOIN HACKHAZARDS ?

  • 💰 Biggest prize pool – $10,000+ in cash, $250,000+ in credits & vouchers
  • 🚀 First-of-its-kind tracks – Monad, Base, Fluvio, Groq & RB Labs – Making their India debut at HACKHAZARDS ‘25!
  • 🌟 Beginner-friendly – Free workshops & bootcamps
  • 🔥 No shortlisting – Everyone gets to hack!
  • ⏰ 210 hours of hacking – Build something amazing
  • 🌍 Global participation – Join from anywhere
  • 🤝 50+ global mentors & judges from top companies
  • 🎁 Swags & cash prizes for Top 100 teams
  • 🎓 Internship & incubation opportunities for standout teams
  • 📜 Certificates for all successful project submissions

… and so much more! 😉

So, register now and let's Build in India, for the World.

Build Your Game, Shape Your World!

GameForge: 36-Hour Gameathon by Realitix Club, Reva University.

GameForge is an electrifying 36-hour Gameathon that unites passionate game developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts to innovate, collaborate, and create immersive gaming experiences. Hosted by Realitix Club, Reva University, this high-energy competition fosters creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving as participants race against time to develop groundbreaking games.

Beyond the competition, GameForge is a hub for learning, networking, and showcasing talent in game development and AR/VR technologies. With industry experts, mentors, and judges, participants gain invaluable insights and exposure. Sponsorship opportunities allow companies to connect with emerging talent, boost brand visibility, and contribute to the future of gaming innovation.

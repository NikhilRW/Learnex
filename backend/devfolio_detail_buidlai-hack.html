About

Welcome to BuidlAI Hackathon

Join us for the inaugural BUIDL AI Hackathon in Seoul, an event dedicated to building autonomous onchain agents to solve real use cases. Hosted by KryptoPlanet, this hackathon kicks off on Friday afternoon, April 11st with an opening ceremony and announcement of winners on April 13rd.

Short Summary of the Event

  • Place: Sky31 Convention
  • Date: APR 11th - 13th
  • Tracks
    • General Track: Build your own
    • Sponsored Track:
  • Programs
    • Opening Ceremony
    • Team Formation / Hacking
    • Mentoring / Workshops
    • Idea Pitch / Judging
    • Award distribution / Closing Ceremony

Caveat

  • Please register as a hacker on Devfolio to participate. Eligible participants will receive confirmation e-mail
  • At least one member of the team must register on site to participate
  • Meals, late night snacks, drinks will be provided for all participants
  • Venue will not be open 24 hours. There will not be any separate accommodation other than the designated venue. Please plan accordingly
